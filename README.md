### one-eyed

you were the moon  
and I, the sun  
one-eyed, broken  
and full of fear  

you took my mind  
and gently kissed  
away all pain,  
every ugly care

and for that  
I praise you  
where the gods seat

in the sky,  
the kingdom of air
